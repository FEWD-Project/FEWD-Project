ABOUT US

Water Equipment Technology is an International Consulting and Design Company specializing in water management and efficient irrigation systems. The company offer water management consulting services to the construction industry, Developers, Landscape Architects and Government bodies. Management and construction of recycled water storage bodies, the management of the eco-balance of lakes and ponds plus the construction and management of wetlands are all vital parts of the experience and expertise of Water Equipment Technology. The staff at WET have eighty years consultancy experience between them, over thirty five years in the industry which gives ability to provide the best level of service in the market place.
